Who is Boderg?

Hi, I'm Simon (aka Boderg). I'm a Junior Full Stack Web Application Developer based in London, UK. I have successfully achieved a Diploma in Full Stack Web Application Development with Code Institute. I love tech (who doesn't?) and I am passionate about building great and visually appealing web sites. I use modern and advanced frameworks and libraries, some of which are listed in my tech stack.
I have worked in the automotive rental industry for 20+ years and I am now forging a new career in web development. I have always enjoyed learning and recently turned my attention toward coding after helping to maintain the websites in my previous employment. Making this career change is helping me achieve my goals and I relish the challenge of learning more along the way.
